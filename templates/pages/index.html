What Are Relevant Implications?

An implication is a possible effect or consequence of your project. For example, if you design a website without considering mobile users, the implication could be that many people can't access it properly. A relevant implication means the effect is directly related to your project, like including a menu on a restaurant website but not sports scores. For NCEA you are required to do a write-up on these.
